"My Doctor" Mobile Application

This is a self-diagnosis mobile application. Users enter their signs and symptoms and in return, the application displays their potential ailment. Additionally, users can search for nearest ambulances and health facilities. During signup, users add next of kin contact details that they are able to cantact during and emergency. Their is also a records section where users can record the hospitals visited and ambulance services used.

"St. Peters, Dioces" Mobile Application (Still building)

This is a church mobile application. Members are able to create an acoount and interact with the application. There is section where they get "Today's reading" which is posted by the admin. Every day, the admin, who is an official of the church, updates the readings. Admin can also post announcements that will be visible to the users. They can join different groups availble and can also give tithe, offering and a donation via the application.
